EEA
===
Verified measurement:
"Data has gone through full quality assurance and quality control by data provider/owner. Data is considered verified and can be formally used for its reporting purposes." [4] [6]

Valid measurement:
Data above detection limit and not due to station maintenance or calibration [5].

Ozone:
Daily maximum of 8h Moving Average (MA), of at least 18 valid 8h MA [3].

PM10:
Daily average (24-hour mean) of at least 18 valid hourly values [1] [2].
